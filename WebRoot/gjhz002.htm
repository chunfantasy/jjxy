Short-term Study Program in Chinese Business for International Students　Study/Research Plan


School of Economics, Wuhan University of Technology

1. Purpose
This program is aiming at providing international students with an intensive training module in specialized courses and internship experience in relating to Chinese economy and business exploration as concerned. Applicants may join in degree courses, research group or in-site investigation for purpose of required understanding of Chinese economic development, business culture and market information.

2. Course study
This program will provide the following courses among which three or four courses must be undertaken for their course study. The results will come into the final record.
Name of Course　　　　　Semester　　　　　Hours　　　Credits　　Language
Introduction to Chinese Culture　1　　　　20　　　　　1　　　　　English
Contemporary Chinese Economic Development 1 40 2 English
Chinese Business Culture 1 40 2 English
Foreign Investment in China 1 40 2 English
Chinese Banking System 1 40 2 English
Seminar in China's Imports and Exports 1 40 2 English

3. Internship
Internship for international students with several channels would be a necessary part of the study program in assistant of the school and Tutors. It is normally placed in later half of the program.
A four-week internship is required. Students should focus on no more than two areas.

4. Teaching and Research Guide
Each student will follow a supervisor or a team to start with their study on campus and practical training experience. Course teaching language is English. The Supervisors are available to conduct this program both in English and Chinese.
The supervisor team is contributed by:

5. Completion of the Program
The completion depends on the implementation of the total scheduled missions and especially the positive evaluation of the counterpart supervisor(s). The length would be individually designed. A final presentation must be raised and got passed from the
Supervisor(s).




外国留学生短期进修中国经济课程项目
学习及研究计划

武汉理工大学经济学院

1. 目的
外国留学生短期学习（经济学）项目旨在给外国留学生集中提供一个与中国经济和市场开发有关的专门课程学习和实践的知识模块。申请者可参加相当学位的课程学习、课题研究或现场调研，以便深入了解必要的中国经济发展现状、企业文化和市场行情。
2. 课程学习
项目提供以下课程。外国留学生可选择3－4门学习。成绩进入结业记录。
课程名称　　　　　学期　　时数 学分 语言
中国文化概论　　　　1　　　20　　1　　　　　英语
当代中国经济发展　　1　　　40　　2　　　　　英语
中国企业文化　　　　1　　　40　　2　　　　　英语
外商在华投资　　　　1 　　 40　　2　　　　　英语
中国银行体系　　　　1 　　 40　　2　　　　　英语
中国进出口贸易专题　1 　　 40　　2　　　　　英语
3. 实习
实习是学习和研究计划中的必要组成部分。实习期为四周，安排在后半期进行。
4. 教学与研究指导
每名留学生由1名导师或一个导师组共同指导。导师（组）主要负责其在校期间的学习和实践环节指导任务。课程采用英语讲授。指导小组由下列教师组成：
魏龙教授 王仁祥教授 聂规划教授 魏建国教授 刘树林教授、陈为教授 凌丹教授 周军教授、胡国晖副教授 黄蕙萍副教授 周毓萍副教授 彭定S副教授
柳哲副教授、寇津春讲师
5. 项目的完成
短期留学生项目的完成要视留学生完成全部规定的学习研究内容而定，需要获得导师评估。留学生于学业完成前需提交报告，事先须获导师同意。
